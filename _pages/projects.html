My Data Mining Side Project

Deep Learning-Based Identification and Extraction of Tomato Leaf Diseases

This project employs data mining techniques for the identification and prediction of tomato diseases.
It is a comprehensive process that primarily involves stages such as data collection, preprocessing, feature engineering, model selection, training, evaluation, optimization, and deployment.
My goal is to construct a robust model that can effectively identify and predict tomato diseases, thereby delivering tangible benefits to the agricultural sector.
